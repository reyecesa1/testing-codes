What Makes NEO Special

NEO isn't just another AI - it's designed to be your personal companion that grows with you.

Two Brains Are Better Than One

NEO has both a chatty side for conversations and a thoughtful side for problem-solving, switching between them automatically based on what you need.

He Actually Remembers

Unlike most AI that forgets everything after your chat, NEO remembers your conversations, preferences, and what you've worked on together.

Gets to Know You

The more you chat with NEO, the better it understands your style, preferences, and how you like to work.

Learns From Mistakes

When something doesn't go quite right, NEO reflects on it and improves for next time - just like a real friend would.

Works Everywhere

Set up NEO on all your devices and it'll keep everything in sync, so you can pick up right where you left off.

Always There For You

Whether you're stuck on a problem at 3 AM or just need someone to bounce ideas off of, NEO is always available.

How NEO Works

Think of NEO as having two specialized brains working together.

The Chatty Friend

This is the part of NEO that's great at conversations - understanding what you're saying, having natural chats, and being emotionally aware.

Powered by SmollM2

The Thoughtful Friend

This is NEO's problem-solving side - great at logic, analysis, and working through complex tasks step by step.

Powered by DeepSeek-R1

Smart Switching

NEO automatically decides which brain to use based on what you're asking - conversations go to the chatty side, complex problems to the thoughtful side.

Auto-Select

Memory That Connects Everything

NEO's memory system keeps track of everything, so both brains have context about your previous conversations and work.

Always Learning

Get Started With NEO

Setting up NEO is easier than you might think. Just follow these steps.

1

Install Ollama

Ollama is like the engine that runs AI models on your computer. It's free and easy to install.

# Download from https://ollama.ai
# Choose your operating system and install
2

Download NEO's Brains

Once Ollama is installed, you need to download NEO's two specialized brains.

# Open terminal/command prompt and run:
ollama pull smollm2
ollama pull deepseek-r1

This might take a few minutes depending on your internet speed.

3

Get the NEO Interface

Download the NEO console interface that brings everything together in one place.

# Download from our website
# This gives you the cool terminal interface
4

Start Chatting!

Run the NEO interface and start having conversations with your new AI friend.

# Double-click the NEO icon
# Or run: ./neo-console

That's it! You're now ready to experience NEO.
